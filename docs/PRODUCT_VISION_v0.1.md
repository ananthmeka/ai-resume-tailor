# Product Vision (v0.1 draft)

## Vision statement

**One profile. Every application, positioned for the role.** An AI career intelligence layer that turns a truthful master resume into job-specific, recruiter-ready documents and transparent improvement guidance — without replacing LinkedIn or job portals.

## Problem

Professionals spend 20–60 minutes per application tailoring resumes, often with weak feedback on why they are not shortlisted. Generic LLMs rewrite text but do not maintain career memory, match scoring, or explain changes against a specific job.

## For whom (initial wedge)

Experienced software professionals (5–25+ years): engineers, leads, architects, engineering managers — high willingness to pay when actively searching.

## Why now

LLMs make parsing and rewriting cheap; job descriptions are more specialized; ATS and keyword filters are stricter. Continuous career management is replacing “update resume only when unemployed.”

## Why us vs ChatGPT / LinkedIn / Naukri

| Alternative | Gap we fill |
|-------------|-------------|
| ChatGPT / Claude | No persistent profile, no structured pipeline, no change log, no match scores tied to a specific JD |
| LinkedIn | Network and listings, not deep JD-specific resume optimization with explainability |
| Job portals | Discovery, not “how to win this application” |

## Success in 3 years

Trusted **AI Career OS**: canonical professional profile, application history, outcome learning, recruiter intelligence (paid side), optional legal job feeds — not “another aggregator.”

## V0.1 scope (this repo)

Free **AI Resume Tailor**: base resume + pasted JD → tailored HTML/PDF, match estimates, missing keywords, change log. No login, no job URL scraping yet, no recruiter billing yet.
