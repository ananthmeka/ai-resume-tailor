Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026. Draft for MVP / beta.

What we collect

When you use AI Resume Tailor, you may upload a resume and paste a job description. These are sent to our API to generate tailored output. The small beta uses an access code to enforce individual usage limits.

How we use data

Data is used only to parse, analyze, and generate resume content for your request. We do not sell personal data.

Storage

Resume content and generated output are processed for the active request and are not intentionally persisted by the application. The service stores only a beta-user identifier, quota counters, and timestamps. Infrastructure and AI-provider technical logs may exist for a limited time.

Third parties

Processing may use an open-weight model through a configured inference provider or a self-hosted model such as Ollama/vLLM. Resume files are not sent to job boards by this application.

Your choices

Do not upload information you are not comfortable sharing with the service operator. You may stop using the service at any time.
